# Employee Leave Policy

## Paid Time Off (PTO)
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per calendar year. 
* PTO does not roll over to the next year.
* Requests for more than 5 consecutive days of PTO must be approved by a direct manager at least two weeks in advance.

## Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually. 
* If you are taking a sick day, please notify your manager and the HR department via email before 9:00 AM local time.
* A doctor's note is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

## Public Holidays
The company observes all federal holidays. If a holiday falls on a weekend, the preceding Friday or following Monday will be given as a day off.
