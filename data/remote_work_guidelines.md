# Remote Work Guidelines

## Core Working Hours
While we offer flexible schedules, all remote employees must be online and available during our core working hours: **10:00 AM to 3:00 PM EST**, Monday through Friday.

## Communication Expectations
* **Urgent issues:** Use Slack and tag the relevant person directly.
* **Non-urgent / Project updates:** Use email or update the relevant Notion page. Asynchronous communication is encouraged.
* **Meetings:** Cameras should be ON for all external client meetings and all-hands company meetings. 

## Home Office Stipend
New remote employees are eligible for a one-time $500 stipend to equip their home office. This can be used for desks, ergonomic chairs, or secondary monitors. Expense receipts must be submitted to finance within your first 30 days.
